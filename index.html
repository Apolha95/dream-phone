How to Play

It's giving mystery, darling. 💛
1
Setup: Add player names, then fill in your crushes — name, photo URL, and tick their traits. Each crush gets a unique dial number shown on their card.
2
A secret admirer is randomly chosen. One crush is secretly picked — no one knows who. That's what you're all racing to figure out!
3
Each player gets 3 cards in hand. Your hand shows which crushes you can call this turn. Pick one card — his number will appear.
4
Dial the number on the phone. After selecting a card, type his 5-digit number on the keypad and press CALL. If the number matches your selected card, he'll answer and give you a clue.
5
Clues are always negative. Clues tell you what the secret admirer is NOT or does NOT have — e.g. "He's not at the gym" or "He doesn't wear a hat." Cross off every boy on the board who has that trait.
6
Tap board cards to eliminate them. Tap any crush on the big board to cross them off. Tap again to unmark them.
7
After calling, your card is replaced. The called card is discarded from your hand and replaced with a new one automatically.
8
Guess when you're ready. Dial 55555 on the phone to make a guess — then pick who you think it is. Right = you win! Wrong = he's eliminated and it's the next player's turn.
9
Pass to the next player using the End Turn button whenever you're done. Players take turns until someone guesses correctly!
Special Cards (each used once per game)
📢 Speakerphone
Use before calling — everyone hears the next clue, not just you.
📵 Mom Says Hang Up!
Play on another player to skip their entire turn.
🤫 Share a Secret
Play when another player gets a clue — they must share it with you.
💡 Tip: Clues always tell you what the admirer is NOT. If someone says "He doesn't hang at the mall," cross off every boy with the mall trait. The last one standing is your secret admirer!
